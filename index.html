Tires

Tire Replacement & Rotation

During tire replacement & rotations our mechanics will:

  • size/speed/load rating of your new tires to make sure they match your door placard.
  • lug nuts and lug studs for excessive wear for your safety. Clean any mating surfaces of rust and corrosion to ensure proper clamping force.
  • Use state of the art wheel service equipment to mount,balance and rotate your tires. We make sure your tires are balanced correctly using the best adapters available.
  • Inform you of any unusual tire wear from your old tires.
  • torque all your lug nuts to factory specification to ensure proper clamping force.

Tire brands offered:

  • Michelin
  • Continental
  • Cooper
  • Yokohama
  • GoodYear
  • Brigestone
  • Firestone
  • General Tire

Smog
